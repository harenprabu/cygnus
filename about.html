Our Story

Cygnus Started in the Spring of Twenty First Century, since the beginning we had the vision to change the traditional approach of managing travel amongst the corporate and to overcome the practical issues that a travel manager faced while handling travel for their company with a tech-savvy approach, where our focal point is to create value for a corporate's time, business & money. With the change in Travel trends, we got an exciting opportunity to adapt and innovate our travel design.
